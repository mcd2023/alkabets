About Us

Our Activities

ALKABETS PHARMACEUTICALS is part of a global, sourcing, import and export healthcare counseling company providing access to overseas formulation, research, development and manufacturing capability for an exhaustive range of products and raw materials. Our business unit is equipped with its 14 qualified experts working in our affiliated Clinical Research Organizations in Berlin, Istanbul and Mumbai for the experimental studies, laboratory tests and compilation of our dossiers.

ALKABETS PHARMACEUTICALS has in its portfolio considerable expertise in providing Drug Technical Development and furnishing Common Technical Documents (CTDs), eCTDs and Finished Formulations. We also provide complete document submission management, developing master submission plans providing a high quality control. This includes managing and coordinating all responses to regulatory authorities’ questions in a timely manner. Whether you’re seeking support for any New Drug Application (NDA) or Marketing Authorization Application, or for post-marketing regulatory compliance filings, let ALKABETS PHARMACEUTICALS handle your documentation and publishing needs
